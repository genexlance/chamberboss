Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the Chamberboss plugin.

General Questions

Can we customize the membership price?

Yes. In the Chamberboss settings, you can set the annual membership price to any amount you choose. You can also select your preferred currency.

How are membership renewals handled?

The plugin automatically sends a renewal reminder email to members before their subscription expires. You can customize the content of this email and how many days in advance it's sent from the settings page.

Can we manually add or edit members?

Absolutely. From the "Members" page in the WordPress admin, you can add new members manually or edit the details of existing members, including their subscription status and expiration date.

Business Directory

Do we have to approve new business listings?

Yes. When a member submits a new business listing, it is set to "Pending" by default. A chamber administrator must review and approve the listing before it appears in the public directory. This gives you full control over the content.

Can we feature certain business listings?

Yes. You can mark any listing as "Featured" from the listing management page. This allows you to highlight specific members in your directory, which can be offered as a premium benefit.

How do we organize the business directory?

You can create custom categories (e.g., "Restaurants," "Retail," "Professional Services") from the "Categories" page. Members can then select a category when submitting their listing, making the directory easy for visitors to navigate.

Payments & Integration

What payment gateways are supported?

Chamberboss integrates exclusively with Stripe, a secure and widely trusted payment processor. This allows you to accept credit card payments for memberships safely and efficiently.

Is it possible to test the payment system?

Yes. The plugin has a "Test Mode" for Stripe. This allows you to simulate transactions and ensure everything is working correctly without processing real payments. We provide detailed instructions for this in our Installation Guide.

Does this plugin work with other WordPress plugins?

Chamberboss is built to standard WordPress coding practices to ensure compatibility. It also includes a direct integration for the popular MailPoet plugin, allowing you to automatically manage your member mailing lists.

Support & Troubleshooting

What if we need help setting up the plugin?

We offer comprehensive documentation, including a detailed Installation & User Guide. If you require hands-on assistance, we also provide premium support and setup services. Please contact us for more information.

What are the technical requirements?

Chamberboss is designed to work with modern WordPress installations. We recommend using the latest version of WordPress and a PHP version of 7.4 or higher for optimal performance and security.
